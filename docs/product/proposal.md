# Product Proposal: Printverse

## BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)
**What:** Printverse is a smart, web-based printer management platform  
**Who:** Designed for student teams, faculty, and organizations with shared printing needs  
**Value:** Minimizes wasted time, document loss, and confusion by automating print job submissions, providing real-time device monitoring, and aggregating actionable analytics

**Primary Metric:**  
Minutes of user time saved per week by replacing manual, repetitive printer checks with automated workflows

---

## Canonical User Story

As a student team member, I want to send a print job, monitor printer status in real-time, and receive a notification when it completes, so I can focus on my work without having to repeatedly check or queue at the device.

### Minimum Acceptance Criteria
- Users can submit print jobs via web/app
- Users can view live printer status and job queue
- System notifies user on job completion or error
- Weekly dashboard shows individual and team productivity stats

---

## KM & Value Chain Analysis

This product delivers measurable impact by:
- Reducing or eliminating manual checks ("Did my print job finish yet?")
- Preventing lost or unclaimed documents (audit trails, alerts)
- Turning tacit usage and frustration into explicit data dashboards
- Enabling teams and admins to see, analyze, and optimize all print activity
- Reducing supply waste and device downtime

**Clear Problem Statement:**  
Teams waste hours each semester waiting for print jobs, seeking help with device issues, or re-printing lost documents. Printverse makes every step transparent, automated, and actionable.
